All names may include special UTF runes provided they are not in the group of whitespace or control
characters. Names may NOT start with a digit from 0 to 9 or a minus sign (‘-’), in addition to theforbidden
runes previously described. A name may also not contain any period (‘.’), quote (‘"’), a curly brace (‘{’ and ‘}’),
a command (‘,’), a semicolon (‘;’) or parentheses (‘(’ and ‘)’).

All names are case sensitive.
